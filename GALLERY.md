## Video Checklist

A concise product video can follow this order:

1. Project and plugin structure.
2. Jet Blueprint component settings.
3. Grounded start, takeoff, and camera modes.
4. HUD, radar, and targeting.
5. Gun, rockets, missiles, and flares.
6. AI dogfight.
7. Mission 03 radio/combat flow.
8. Private multiplayer.
9. Public EOS setup boundary and a tested internet session.
10. Settings, profile persistence, respawn, spectating, and killer card.

## Media Guidelines

- Capture the actual Unreal Engine 5.7 project or its packaged build.
- Use consistent 16:9 framing where possible.
- Hide debug text unless the image documents diagnostics.
- Avoid showing EOS secrets, product IDs, account email addresses, or access tokens.
- Label editor-only examples clearly.
- Keep images compressed enough for fast GitHub page loading.

## Project Scope Reminder

The gallery should not advertise dedicated servers, CTF, King of the Hill, ranked progression, achievements, fuel simulation, voice chat, or aircraft/weapons that are not included.
